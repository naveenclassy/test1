About

AAMEC constantly strives for excellence. We, at AAMEC aim at ensuring that the students who bear the seal of AAMEC are capable of competing in any global context with success and distinction. Located in a sprawling campus of about 65 acres of land at the historic site of Kovilvenni.

Our vision

To become a centre of excellence by imparting high quality technical, education, and produce competent engineers with ethical, environmental and social values.

Our mission

  • Facilitating high quality technical education through adequate infrastructure and eminent faculty members.

  • Providing technical education to rural youth

  • Promoting research and development activities

  • Developing industry – Institution interaction and inculcating a spirit of entrepreneurship through sustained efforts.

  • Creating global environmental awareness among the students.

Undergraduate programs

B.E

Computer Science and Engineering

To apply the knowledge gained in mathematics, basic sciences, and core concepts of computer science and engineering in real-time applications.

B.Tech

Chemical Enginnering

Apply the knowledge of mathematics and engineering sciences to design and analyze products and processes related to chemical engineering systems.

B.E

Electrical and Electronics Engineering

Apply the knowledge of mathematics and engineering sciences to design and analyze products and processes related to electrical and electronics engineering systems.

B.E

Electronics and Communication Engineering

To enable graduates to pursue research, or have a successful career in academia or industries associated with Electronics and Communication Engineering, or as entrepreneurs.

B.Tech

Information Technology

To ensure graduates will be proficient in utilizing the fundamental knowledge of basic sciences, mathematics, and Information Technology for the applications relevant to various streams of Engineering.

B.E

Mechanical Engineering

Practice mechanical engineering in general streams including thermal design, manufacturing, service, and allied engineering sectors.

B.E

Civil Engineering

Prepare students for successful careers in civil engineering fields that meet the needs of Indian and multinational companies.

Master’s programs

Master of Computer Applications

Prepare students to excel in the computing profession by providing solid technical foundations in the field of computer applications.
